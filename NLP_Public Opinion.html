Aiding Tuition Promotion Strategy

Due to the fact that online studying hindered student's learning ability, thus tuition is certainly more favorable, especially if it is done directly(offline).

January 28, 2022

Problems

Offline studying policy in Indonesia had been ordered to school in the early month of January in the year 2022, which caused several debate about such policy. According to previous statement, our client which run in tuition services requested the aid of out services to provide sentiment analysis. Thus it is essential to understand the perspective of society about it to formulize some kind of promotion strategy.

Features

PTM: An offline learning policy used as a keyword to web scrape from Twitter and news website (Kompas.com)

Download Dataset

Analysis

Analysis of the sentiment was targeted 3 days prior (from 23 January 2022 until 26 January 2022), resulting in 345 row of data, consisting of 300 tweets and 45 news title.

Overall distribution of data

Based on data distribution, overall tweet and news title length are short, with no longer than 50 characters and no more than 16 sentences, indicating the tendency of short and simple way of sharing information.

Overall common word distribution and combination

PTM has the highest count based on the methodology of using that word as a keyword, resulting in the highest number. To further understand the sentiment of each of the tweets and news title, machine learning was used to conduct story building and context understanding. Based on the machine learning, we discovered there were 4 context, that is overall reffering to the increasing numbers of offline learning fro school and also encouragement for students, teachers, and parents.

Sentiment analysis

Based on the overall context, there is support regarding the offline learning policy. For the sentiment analysis itself, data shown an average sentiment of 0 toward this topic. This result means that within the range of sampling days (3 days), people tend to be neutral. Neutrality maybe due to several factors, such as a majority of people is not affected by situation (for example, not having children).

For the purpose of the project, we must consider the positive and negative perspective as well. Based on the graph above, it is shown that positive response has a higher value comparing to the negative one. The result provide a promising view of expanding the offline tuition promotion toward the latter months.
